## 1. Introduction
Nova Financial Services provides technology resources to support business operations. Misuse of these assets may expose the company to risk. This policy outlines acceptable and unacceptable use.

## 2. Scope
Applies to all employees, contractors, and third parties using Nova systems or data.

## 3. Acceptable Use
- Use systems for business purposes  
- Protect credentials  
- Report incidents  
- Use approved software only  
- Follow data handling guidelines  

## 4. Unacceptable Use
- Bypassing security controls  
- Excessive personal use  
- Sharing sensitive data externally  
- Installing unapproved software  
- Connecting unauthorized devices  

## 5. Enforcement
Violations may result in disciplinary action or termination.
